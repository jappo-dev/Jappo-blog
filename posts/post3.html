#3 Start working on projects!

Posted by Jappo on December 5, 2021

Bootstrap

As I may have mentioned before, I am participating in the web development bootcamp of Angela Yu (which I may highly recommend). I have covered the basics of Web development, debugging, HTML and CSS and a couple of days ago we started on frameworks. The first one is bootstrap, a front-end framework that enables you to build websites fast and easy. Great for easy learning and the documentation on it is top notch. Using this, I have created some static webpages and started working on my first project for a friend. I am noticing that my pace is quite slow, my pages are very unoptimzed but it is so much fun to learn and explore by doing instead of just listening to tuturials all day.

100 day coding challenge

I must admit that I have not been keeping up with it as much as I would have liked but progress is definitely being made. One of the things I do is to dip myself in the world of web development by connecting with people through social media, reading books and even listening to podcasts on the subject. This really keeps me motivated and engaged on the topic.
