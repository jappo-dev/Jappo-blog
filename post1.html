About me

Hi there! Thanks for reading my first blogpost. My Name is Jasper Pastoor, I am a 25 year old student from the Netherlands and currently working on finishing my master thesis for my Business Administrion study. In the meantime I love working on web development, it's something that I have been interested in for a long time but only since the pandemic started have I made the conscious effort and dedication to start learning it.

A little but more about who I am: I love sports, programming and of course having the occasional drink. I grew up in a small town in the Netherlands but have since them moved to the big city and I couldn't be happier about that. Life is moving much faster here but that's what makes it much more interesting, for me at least.

My goals

Web development has been a hobby but so far I haven't really thought about what I want to accomplish with it. One of the dreams I have had since I was a little kid was to start my own company. Perhaps web development is the right space for that... only time will tell. For now I have been following the web development bootcamp on Udemy (great website for checking out a lot of useful courses), been connecting with the techside of Twitter (bunch of great guys and gals on there) and I have been asking friends and family for their view on web development and if they needed help with some of their projects. And finally, I started this Blog. I created the webspace for it and will be updating it as regularly as possible.

100 day coding challenges

So one of the main challenges involving coding and web development these days is the so-called 100 days coding challenge. Most of you might be familiar with this and I would love to give it a shot as long as it does not interfere with my study duties.
